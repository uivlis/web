Gitcoin is a platform for you to discover freelance programming jobs and contribute to open bug bounties or software development requests.

All work is done over Github to make showing your work and communicating with project owners as simple as possible.

There are open issues & bug bounties in our Issue Explorer for {% for subject in contributor_list %} {{subject.text}}, {% endfor %} and much more.

Gitcoin’s mission is to grow open source and, by contributing to top OSS projects’ code, you are able to get paid for your freelance programming and connect with OSS repo owners and likeminded coders from around the world.
